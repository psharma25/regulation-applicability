What exists instead of a trade feed

There is no dated weekly buy/sell stream for the President or Cabinet. They file annual OGE Form 278e disclosures — assets and income in broad ranges. Anyone selling a "President's weekly stock picks" feed is inferring from delayed annual snapshots or fabricating.
