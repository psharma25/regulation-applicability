Defensive threat intelligence · scoring workbook + live tracker

Threat actor reputation/board

Ranks ransomware & extortion actors on two axes — how dangerous they are, and whether they honor a payment — from analyst priors enriched with live public reporting.

This is the static showcase. It displays the preloaded roster with scores computed from analyst priors, so it runs anywhere with no backend. The full agentic scan — live evidence collection, new-actor discovery, local-LLM reasoning, and Excel export — runs in the Python app: pip install -r requirements.txt then uvicorn app.main:app.
Threat severity — higher is worse for you Deal reliability — higher means more likely to honor payment State-directed — sanctions risk on payment

Scores are a planning aid, not an endorsement — every listed actor is a criminal operation. "First seen" is the year the group was first publicly observed; reported victims show the incident year and are well-known public attributions, illustrative not exhaustive. Ransoms actually paid and payer identities are mostly undisclosed; reliability reflects reputation and incentive structure, not a verified success rate.
