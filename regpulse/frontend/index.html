How To Use RegPulse

Five capabilities, one workflow. Start by loading an example or entering your own product.

1 · Find what applies
Go to Applicability. Pick a company/product or example, or enter your own markets and data, then run it to see what applies and why, with priorities.
2 · Read each regulation + source
In Regulations, open any card for the summary, the official source link, the latest change, and a roadmap download (HTML or Excel).
3 · Track weekly change
In Weekly Delta, run a scan to see new / updated / unchanged regulations with impact, and download the delta report (HTML or Excel).
4 · Build the roadmap
After applicability, download a single combined, de-duplicated roadmap across all applicable regulations (HTML or Excel).
5 · Get the controls
In Controls, each control has an explanation, action steps, priority and severity. Download one or the full library.
