# Security Incident Management - RACI Matrix

Detection through post-incident review (ISO 27001 A.5.24-A.5.28; NIST SP 800-61).

**Legend:** R = Responsible, A = Accountable, C = Consulted, I = Informed

| Activity | Incident Manager | CISO | SecOps / Analyst | Business Owner | Legal / Privacy | Comms / Exec |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Detect & report event | A | I | R | C | I | I |
| Triage & classify severity | A | C | R | C | I | I |
| Declare major incident | R | A | C | C | C | I |
| Contain & eradicate | A | C | R | C | I | I |
| Breach / regulatory assessment | C | A | C | C | R | I |
| External & customer comms | C | C | I | C | A | R |
| Recover & verify | A | C | R | R | I | I |
| Post-incident review & CAPA | R | A | C | C | C | I |
