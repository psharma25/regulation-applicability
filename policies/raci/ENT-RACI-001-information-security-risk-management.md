# Information Security Risk Management - RACI Matrix

Identification, assessment, treatment and acceptance of information-security risk (ISO 27001 Cl.6.1, 8.2, 8.3).

**Legend:** R = Responsible, A = Accountable, C = Consulted, I = Informed

| Activity | Risk / System Owner | CISO | Risk & Compliance | Asset Owner | Internal Audit | Executive Mgmt |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Maintain risk methodology & criteria | C | A | R | I | C | I |
| Identify & log risks | R | C | A | R | I | I |
| Assess likelihood & impact | R | C | A | C | I | I |
| Select & plan risk treatment | A | C | R | C | I | I |
| Approve residual-risk acceptance | C | R | C | I | I | A |
| Track treatment to closure | R | A | R | C | I | I |
| Report risk posture to leadership | I | R | A | I | C | I |
