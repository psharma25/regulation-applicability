# Medical Device Regulatory Submissions (reference)

## US FDA
510(k) premarket notification demonstrates substantial equivalence to a predicate; mandatory via eSTAR. De Novo classifies a novel low/moderate-risk device with no predicate (eSTAR-mandatory). PMA is for high-risk Class III devices requiring clinical evidence. IDE permits an investigational clinical study. Pre-Submission (Q-Sub) obtains FDA feedback. Section 524B requires cybersecurity documentation for cyber devices: security risk management report, machine-readable SBOM, secure product development framework, and a postmarket cybersecurity management plan. AI/ML devices may use a Predetermined Change Control Plan (PCCP) to pre-authorize defined model updates.

## EU MDR / IVDR
The central artifact is the Technical Documentation (Annex II/III): GSPR checklist, clinical/performance evaluation report, risk management file (ISO 14971), PMS/PMCF plans, and UDI. A Notified Body performs conformity assessment leading to CE marking and a Declaration of Conformity. EUDAMED registration applies. Transition deadlines under Regulation 2023/607 extend to 2027/2028 by risk class.

## Other markets
UK uses UKCA marking via UK Approved Bodies and MHRA registration. Canada requires a Medical Device Licence with MDSAP certification. Japan (PMDA) uses Todokede/Ninsho/Shonin by class. China NMPA requires local testing and often local clinical data. Australia TGA uses ARTG inclusion. Brazil ANVISA requires registration and B-GMP. MDSAP is a single audit accepted by US, Canada, Australia, Japan and Brazil. IMDRF provides harmonized frameworks for SaMD, cybersecurity and RWE.

## Recurring artifacts
Device description and intended use, predicate/equivalence comparison, classification rationale, GSPR/essential principles checklist, risk management file (ISO 14971), software documentation (IEC 62304) with SBOM, cybersecurity threat model (AAMI TIR57, ISO 81001-5-1), usability/human factors (IEC 62366), biocompatibility (ISO 10993), electrical safety (IEC 60601), clinical/performance evaluation, labeling/IFU, and verification & validation protocols and reports.
